Need for Speed: Most Wanted (2005)

by EERIE

14 charts covering all 32 cars, ranked by Top Speed, Acceleration, and Handling, individually and in every combination.

Both stock and fully tuned stats are included, plus the best car for each Blacklist rival (stacked Top Speed + Acceleration + Handling), based on the car availability plus available performance upgrades present at the time of each blacklist race.

Stock values are taken directly from nfs.fandom.com (rated 0–10).
Fully tuned values I calculated manually. The game stores each stat internally as a float between -44 and 116, which I scanned with Cheat Engine and converted to a 0–100% scale.
